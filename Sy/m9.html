Getting Outsmarted By The CEO

Author: 车厘酒

Genre:Comedy, Romance

Type:Chinese Web Novel

Chapter: 101

Status: Completed

SUMMARY:

Ruan An’an, who had a net worth in the tens of billions, returned to her alma mater to donate a building and fell in love with a college student there.

The boy wore a washed-out shirt. His figure was slender and tall. With black hair, light-colored pupils, and an elegant jawline, he looked incredibly beautiful.

Ruan An’an was dazzled by his charms and accidentally bumped into him. The college student picked up the files she dropped and smiled, “fellow student, watch your step.”

His voice was as clear as spring water and his person as handsome as jade.

For the first time in her life, Ruan An’an was struck by love.

She created an opportunity for herself by changing her identity.

As the story goes, a sweet girl with financial difficulties, who constantly strived for self-improvement, quickly fell in love with a poor-yet-handsome male college student. The two were in tune with each other in all aspects and got married in just half a year.

Although they were hard-pressed for money even after marriage, it did not affect their relationship.

On the night before their wedding anniversary, Ruan An’an had changed in one of her exquisite business attires at work, as usual, and went to attend a private meeting with the so-called “mysterious major shareholder”.

“Ms. Ruan,” the secretary introduced: “This is Mr. Gu, Gu Jue.”

Ruan An’an stared at the man whom she shared a bed with in a 100-square-meter apartment every night. He was impeccably dressed, looking very sharp and honorable.

…She almost broke her high heels.

“An’an,” He said with a warm smile that melted his iciness: “Let’s move to Lin Jiang Mansion, I built a garden for you.”

Ruan An’an: “…”

Damn you! What “poor-yet-handsome college student”? It was all a set up!
